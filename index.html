Math operations

Mathematical operations cover the calculations that are used for solving the equations.

Addition

Addition is one of the basic operations of arithmetic. In its simplest form, the addition combines two numbers into a single number.

Subtraction

Subtraction is one of four basic mathematical operations in which, for each two values, one is subtracted from the other, that is, one quantity is taken from another, and the remaining value is the result of that operation.

Multiplication

Multiplication is a natural evolution of addition, because it is defined to represent the sum of a certain number of sets that have the same number of elements.
